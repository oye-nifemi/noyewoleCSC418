Dr Ikechukwu Obiaya

Dr Ikechukwu Obiaya the Dean of the School of Media and Communication, Pan-Atlantic University. He has an impressive background and extensive experience in academia. 
He is passionate about imparting knowledge and has lectured at the University for over 15 years. He received his PhD in Communication from the University of Navarra. 
Before this recent appointment, Dr Obiaya was the Director of PhD Programmes at the School of Media and Communication. He has also served the School in different capacities.
